üìã Dashboard Flow

Stage 1: Welcome & Selection

Users are greeted and choose their preferred test length:

  • 12 flips: Quick test (~3 minutes)
  • 20 flips: Standard test (~5 minutes)
  • 50 flips: Challenging test (~7 minutes)
  • 200 flips: Expert test (~10 minutes)

Stage 2: Mental Coin Flipping

Users imagine flipping a coin and enter their sequence:

  • Real-time character counter: 12 / 20 characters
  • Input validation (only H and T allowed)
  • Visual feedback on completion status

Seven cognitive reflection questions with free-text input:

  • Bat and ball problem
  • Widget machines
  • Lily pads doubling
  • Race position
  • Sheep problem
  • Emily's sisters
  • Dirt in a hole
